Frequently asked questions

How quickly does MyHR start contacting workers after a call-out?

Within seconds. As soon as a shift opens, MyHR begins reaching out to every eligible worker simultaneously via call, text, and messaging apps.

Can we set rules for who gets contacted first?

Absolutely. You can prioritize by seniority, proximity, skill set, overtime status, or any custom rule your team needs.

What happens if multiple workers respond?

MyHR automatically ranks responses based on your rules and confirms the best match. Runners-up are released instantly so they aren't left waiting.

Does MyHR integrate with our existing scheduling system?

Yes. MyHR connects with all major workforce management and scheduling platforms including Kronos, ADP, Deputy, and more.

Can our team still be involved in the process if needed?

Of course. MyHR keeps your coordinators in the loop with real-time updates and lets them override or step in at any point.

How much time does this actually save?

Most teams report going from 30+ minutes per shift fill down to under 5 minutes — freeing up hours every single day.

Can I tailor MyHR to our team's communication style?

Yes. You can customize scripts, tone, and language. MyHR can also learn from past conversations to better match how your team communicates.
