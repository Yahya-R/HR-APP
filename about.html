Our Story

MyHR started with a 4 AM phone call.

In 2023, our co-founders Amir Kassar and Rachel Drummond were running operations at two different staffing agencies — Amir in industrial staffing in Ohio, Rachel in logistics staffing in Texas. They didn't know each other yet, but they were living the same nightmare every morning: phones ringing before dawn, coordinators scrambling to fill call-out shifts, clients threatening to pull contracts, and a constant churn of burned-out scheduling staff.

The breaking point for Amir came when his best coordinator — a woman who'd been with the agency for six years — quit on a Tuesday morning, citing the 4 AM calls as the reason she couldn't do it anymore. "She wasn't bad at her job," Amir says. "Her job was bad at being a job."

Rachel's moment was different. She watched her team spend 340 hours in a single month making phone calls that went to voicemail. "We were paying people to leave messages that nobody listened to. That's not a staffing operation — that's a broken system."

They met at SIA's Staffing Industry Executive Forum in early 2024 and realized they were describing the same problem from different ends of the country. By March, they'd recruited a founding engineering team and started building MyHR — an AI system that could do the repetitive outreach work instantly and simultaneously, so human coordinators could focus on the work that actually requires a human.

Today, MyHR fills thousands of shifts every week for staffing agencies across the U.S. Our AI agents handle the initial outreach — calls, texts, and messages — while coordinators focus on building relationships with workers and clients. The shifts get filled faster, the coordinators don't burn out, and the agencies grow.

What We Believe

Workers First

Every feature we build gets evaluated through the lens of the worker experience. If it makes life worse for workers, it doesn't ship — even if agencies would buy it.

Replace Tasks, Not People

AI should eliminate the work that burns people out, not the people doing the work. Our best customers have zero layoffs — they have happier, more productive teams.

Earn Trust with Data

We handle sensitive worker information. SOC 2 Type II is our baseline, not our ceiling. We're transparent about what we collect, why, and how long we keep it.

Ship and Measure

We release weekly, measure everything, and share the results publicly in our engineering changelog. If something doesn't move the needle, we kill it.
