Getting Started

How long does onboarding take?

Most agencies are fully live within 5-7 business days. The process includes: connecting your scheduling system (Day 1-2), configuring contact rules and worker eligibility criteria (Day 2-3), running parallel testing alongside your existing process (Day 3-5), and going live with monitoring (Day 5-7). Enterprise deployments with custom integrations may take 2-3 weeks.

What scheduling systems does MyHR integrate with?

We have native, bidirectional integrations with Bullhorn, ADP Workforce Now, UKG Pro (formerly UltiPro), Deputy, and Kronos Workforce Ready. We also support any system with a REST API through our custom connector framework. If your system isn't listed, contact us — we add new integrations regularly based on customer demand.

Do we need to change our existing workflow?

No. MyHR is designed to plug into your current process, not replace it. When a shift needs to be filled, MyHR handles the initial outreach and response collection. Your coordinators still make the final decisions and handle exceptions. Most teams describe it as "removing the phone tag" from their existing workflow.

Integrations

How does the Bullhorn integration work?

MyHR connects to Bullhorn via the REST API using OAuth 2.0. We sync worker profiles, job orders, placements, and availability in real time. When a shift is filled through MyHR, the placement is automatically created in Bullhorn with full audit trail. Changes made in either system sync within 30 seconds.

Can I use MyHR with multiple scheduling systems?

Yes. Some agencies use different systems for different client sites or divisions. MyHR can connect to multiple systems simultaneously and route shift data to the correct integration. Worker profiles are deduplicated across systems using phone number and email matching.

What data does MyHR need access to?

At minimum: worker contact information (phone/email), shift schedules, and worker availability. For full functionality, we also use: certifications and compliance data (for eligibility filtering), worker location or zip code (for proximity-based contact), and historical shift acceptance patterns (for preference learning). All data access follows the principle of least privilege.

Billing

How is MyHR priced?

MyHR is priced per filled shift — you only pay when MyHR successfully fills a shift. There are no per-seat fees, no per-worker fees, and no minimum commitments on our standard plan. Enterprise pricing with volume discounts and custom SLAs is available for agencies filling 500+ shifts per week. Contact sales for a quote.

Are there any setup or onboarding fees?

No. Onboarding, integration setup, and training are included at no additional cost. Our white-glove onboarding team handles the entire setup process. Enterprise customers with custom integration requirements may have a one-time professional services fee — this is discussed and agreed upon before signing.

What happens if I need to cancel?

You can cancel at any time with 30 days' notice on the standard plan. There are no early termination fees. We'll export all your data in a standard format (CSV + JSON) and provide a 90-day data retention window in case you change your mind. Enterprise contracts may have different terms — check your agreement.

Technical

How does MyHR handle worker consent for AI calls?

MyHR includes a consent management system that tracks opt-in status for every worker. During onboarding, we help you update your worker enrollment paperwork to include TCPA-compliant consent language for AI-assisted communications. Workers can opt out at any time by replying STOP to any text or pressing a key during any call. Opt-outs are enforced immediately and system-wide.

What happens during an outage or downtime?

MyHR is built on redundant infrastructure across two AWS regions with automatic failover. In the unlikely event of a complete outage, all pending outreach is queued and automatically resumes when service is restored. We also send immediate email and SMS alerts to your designated contacts so your team can activate manual backup procedures. Our historical uptime is 99.97%.

Can we access the MyHR API directly?

Yes. The MyHR API is available to all customers. Full documentation is at docs.myhr.ai. We provide official SDKs for Python and Node.js, with Go and Java coming Q2 2026. API access includes shift management, worker management, reporting, webhook configuration, and outreach control. Rate limits are generous: 1,000 requests/minute on standard plans, 10,000/minute on enterprise.
